Course outline (early draft)

  • Four methods: GOFAI, control theoryish, reinforcement learning, function approximators (e.g. NNs)
  • Two tasks: Tic-tac-toe, cruise control
    • One final boss: PONG

Learning goals and apporach

  • General understanding of how to formalize and implement Concepts
  • Understanding through implementation
  • Students implement and tweak the "critical bits" of the methods
    • No extensive technical knowledge needed, we'll handle the cruft
  • Not a cookbook!

Cognitive modeling (in this course)

    Mechanistic dynamic models. I.e. robots.

    But not robotics. We aren't interested in making robots, but in understanding why intelligent systems work or don't work.
